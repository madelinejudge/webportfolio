  • Lansing Roots Brochure

  • As part of an internship with the Greater Lansing Food Bank's Lansing Roots program, I created this brochure explaining the concept and details of their Community Supported Agriculture program. I created this brochure using Microsoft Word so that it would be compatible with my supervisor's computer. Due to budget restrictions, the brochure had to have few colors and be able to be printed in-house. I included professional photographs and the organization's logo colors, creating an aesthetically-pleasing design with low printing costs.

  • Feeding the Future UURAF Poster

  • As part of a funded grant, I presented my research project, Feeding the Future, at the University Undergraduate Research and Arts Forum. I designed my poster using Microsoft PowerPoint and used photos from Twitter and Facebook. I also wrote all of the content, pulling themes from my research analysis.
